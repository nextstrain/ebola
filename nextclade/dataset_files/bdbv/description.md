Data from the 2026 outbreak were generously shared by the groups of Prof. Placide Mbala-Kingebeni (INRB, DRC) and Dr Isaac Ssewanyana (CPHL, Uganda) to facilitate the public health response and containment of the virus. These data are described in a post on [Virological.org](https://virological.org/t/initial-genomes-from-may-2026-bundibugyo-virus-disease-outbreak-in-the-democratic-republic-of-the-congo-and-uganda/1032) and were deposited in Pathoplexus under Restricted Data-Use terms.
